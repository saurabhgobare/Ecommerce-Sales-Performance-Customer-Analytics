# Business Questions Addressed

This document outlines the key business questions answered using Power BI dashboards
and interactive analytics.

---

## 1. Executive & Sales Performance
1. How are overall revenue, profit, and order volume trending over time?
2. What are the year-over-year and month-over-month changes in revenue, orders, and returns?
3. How accurately do current monthly metrics perform against previous month performance?
4. Which product categories generate the highest number of orders?

---

## 2. Product Performance & Returns
5. Which products generate the highest revenue, profit, and order volume?
6. Which products and categories have the highest return rates?
7. Which products identified in the **Executive Dashboard** require deeper
   investigation, and how does **drill-through navigation** enable
   detailed product-level performance analysis?
8. How do product revenue, profit, orders, and return rates trend over time
   (year, month, week)?

---

## 3. Customer Analytics
9. How many unique customers are active each year?
10. What is the average revenue per customer?
11. How do customer orders vary by income level and occupation?
12. Who are the top-performing customers by revenue and order volume?
13. How does customer engagement trend by year, month, and week?

---

## 4. Regional & Geographic Insights
14. Which continents and regions generate the highest total order volume?
15. How does regional performance compare using geographic visualization?

---

## 5. Target & Performance Monitoring
16. How do monthly orders, revenue, and profit compare against predefined targets?
17. Which KPIs require corrective action based on underperformance?

---

## Key Outcome
These business questions support **executive decision-making**, **product optimization**,
**customer strategy**, and **regional growth planning** using interactive dashboards and
drill-through analysis.

# KPI Definitions

This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) used across all dashboards.

---

## Core Sales KPIs
- **Revenue:** Total sales value generated from completed orders.
- **Profit:** Revenue minus cost (as provided in the dataset).
- **Orders:** Total number of customer orders placed.
- **Return Rate (%):** Percentage of orders that were returned.
- **Revenue per Customer:** Total revenue divided by unique customers.

---

## Time-Based Performance KPIs
- **Monthly Revenue:** Revenue generated in the selected month.
- **Monthly Orders:** Orders placed during the selected month.
- **Monthly Returns:** Returned orders during the selected month.
- **Previous Month Comparison:** Comparison of current month KPIs with previous month values.
- **Trend Analysis:** KPI movement across year, month, and week.

---

## Product & Return KPIs
- **Total Quantity Sold:** Total number of units sold per product.
- **Product Revenue Contribution:** Revenue generated by individual products.
- **Product Return Rate:** Return percentage at the product level.
- **Category Return Rate:** Return percentage aggregated by product category.

---

## Customer KPIs
- **Unique Customers:** Count of distinct customers.
- **Top Customer (Revenue):** Customer with the highest revenue contribution.
- **Customer Orders:** Total orders placed by each customer.

---

## Target-Based KPIs
- **Orders vs Target:** Actual monthly orders compared with target.
- **Revenue vs Target:** Actual monthly revenue compared with target.
- **Profit vs Target:** Actual monthly profit compared with target.

---

## Drill-Through KPI Context
- **Product-Level Performance:**  
  Detailed analysis of revenue, profit, orders, returns, and return rate
  accessed through **drill-through navigation** from the Executive Dashboard
  to the Product Performance Dashboard.

---

## KPI Purpose
These KPIs are designed to provide **clear, actionable insights** for
executive monitoring, operational optimization, and strategic decision-making.

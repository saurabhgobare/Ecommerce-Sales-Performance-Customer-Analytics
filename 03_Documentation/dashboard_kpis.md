# Dashboard KPIs & Business Metrics Documentation

This document explains the KPIs, slicers, and business metrics used across all Power BI dashboards
in the **E-Commerce Sales Performance & Customer Analytics** project.

## üìä Dashboard 1: Executive Dashboard

### Global Filters
- Year
- Continent
- Reset Button
- Page Navigation Icons

### KPI Cards
| KPI | Description |
|----|-------------|
| Revenue | Total sales value generated |
| Orders | Total number of orders |
| Profit | Revenue minus cost |
| Return % | Percentage of returned orders |

### Visuals & Metrics
- **Line Chart:** Revenue trend with forecasting
- **Stacked Bar Chart:** Total orders by category
- **Matrix:** Top 10 products by Revenue, Profit, Orders
- **Monthly KPIs:**
  - Current Month Revenue vs Previous Month
  - Current Month Orders vs Previous Month
  - Current Month Returns vs Previous Month
- **Text Cards:**
  - Most ordered product type
  - Most returned product type

### Business Value
- Helps executives track overall business health
- Identifies growth trends and seasonality
- Supports forecasting and performance monitoring
